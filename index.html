Three Yoga Poses While Pregnant

Sukhasana

This is an easy pose while pregnant. It helps to align your spin, reduces back pain, and can relax anxiety.

Hip Circles

This pose helps your lower back. Get on your knees, rest your arms on the ball, and rotate your hips clock ways and counter clock ways.

Side to Side

This pose helps with better posture. It's also good to help stretch out arms.
